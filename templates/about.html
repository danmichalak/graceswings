What is Grace's Wings?
Grace’s Wings is a Supernatural/Urban Fantasy webcomic about a for-hire exorcist and the demonic voice in her head. While on what looks like a run-of-the-mill haunting case, she uncovers a prophecy that details the return of a powerful and malevolent entity that was thought destroyed decades ago. However, she isn’t the only one who knows of the prophecy. Shadowy figures work in the darkness, each with different plans for the prophecy (and Nadia) in mind.
When do you update?
Every week on Mondays and Wednesdays, except for holidays and catastrophes. We also take a week off between chapters.
